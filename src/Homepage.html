Chapter I

The Golden Silk of Cambodia

Unique to Cambodia, the Golden Silk is spun from yellow cocoons, yielding a fiber of extraordinary luster and natural resilience. This rare material has dressed Khmer royalty for centuries, representing the pinnacle of luxury and spiritual purity.

Unlike modern mass-produced fabrics, each thread is hand-reeled, a painstaking process that requires patience and an intimate understanding of the silkworm's lifecycle. It is a slow art form in a fast world.
