Our Story

From the Golden Age of Angkor to a Modern Revival.

For centuries, Cambodian silk was the fabric of divinity, adorning the walls of Angkor and the bodies of kings. Khmer Atelier was founded to ensure this golden thread was never broken. Following years of silence, we sought out the last remaining master weavers in remote provinces, reclaiming techniques that were nearly lost to history.

Today, we stand as a bridge. We honor the meticulous, slow-fashion approach of our ancestors while embracing the contemporary silhouettes of today's global elite. Every garment is a living archive, a narrative of resilience, and a celebration of Cambodian identity.
