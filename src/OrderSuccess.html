The Alchemy of Silk

01

Heritage Threads

We source our silk exclusively from the Golden Silk Farm in Banteay Srei, where rare golden silkworms produce naturally pigmented yellow thread, eliminating the need for synthetic dyes.

02

The Weaving Rhythm

Using a multi-shaft loom, our artisans weave complex 'Pidan' patterns that represent spiritual protection and abundance, a tradition passed down through seven generations.

03

Metallic Finishing

Fine silver threads coated in 22k gold are hand-embroidered into the borders of the Sampot, creating a subtle luminescence that shifts beautifully with movement.
